Adventure Through Princeton

A Familiar Approach to COS126

made by: Handa Chun

How to Play

This game is pretty easy to play.

You control the character on the screen using the arrow keys (←, ↑, ↓, →) to move across the Princeton Campus to discover different opponents in each of the different building.

When you "bump" into an opponent (who are supposed to be professors and famous computer-related figures), you will be greeted by a COS 126 question on the screen. If you answer the question correctly, you will see your grade go up. The highest grade you can get to is "The GoAT"

To answer the questions, simply click on the correct answer!
